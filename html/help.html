Search:

How To Use:

To use the search feature, you first need to click into the search box. Then you need to type in an ingredient into the search box that you would like to search up. Then click the search button on the right or press enter. If there are drinks that have that ingredient you searched, they will be displayed below. You can scroll if there are a lot of drinks that have that ingredient or you can choose in the settings, how many drinks to display on the screen. You can also click on the drink to preview a picture of it and a description on how to make it.

FAQs:

Q: How do I save drinks for later?

A: You will press the flag in the top right of the drink display so that the color is purple, which means that drink is saved for later.

Q: Can I search up more than one ingredient at a time?

A: No, you can not search up more than one ingredient at a time.

Q: What if there are no results when I search up my ingredient?

A: That is because there are no drinks containing that ingredient in our database.

Saved:

How To Use:

There are multiple ways to use the Saved page. You can edit your saved drinks by removing them from your saved by clicking the purple flag in the top right. You can also search from your list of saved drinks. All you have to do is click into the search box and type in an ingredient you would like to search for. Then you can hit enter or the search icon on the right. You can also click on the saved drink to see a picture and a description on how to make it. If you search up an ingredient and there are no drinks that appear below, that means you have not saved any drinks with that ingredient.

FAQs:

Q: Why did no drinks come up after I searched for an ingredient?

A: The reason no drinks came up when searching that ingredient, is because you have no drinks saved that have that ingredient.

Q: How do I go back to see all of my saved drinks?

A: All you need to do is go to the search box and click into it. Then delete the searched ingredient from the search box and then hit enter or the search icon on the right.

Q: How do I remove saved drinks?

A: To remove saved drinks, just click the purple flag in the top right, and it will get removed from your saved.

Help:

How To Use:

You can use the scroll bar on the right to go up and down to view the help page that includes helpful information about the other pages. The information that you can see about the other pages is how to use each page. You can also see FAQs about each page with answers right below to the questions.

Key Board Short Cuts:

If you press the alt + 1 brings you to the Search Page

If you press the alt + 2 brings you to the Saved Page

If you press the alt + 3 brings you to the Help Page

If you press the alt + S brings up the Settings

Note: settings are stored locally in your browser and are specific to this site (they won't transfer between different domains or devices).
